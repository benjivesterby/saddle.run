Community Events

HOA Meetings

The Saddle Run Homeowner’s Association (HOA) Meetings will typically be held on the 1st Wednesday of each month, subject to change as needed. Please check back often for the location and specific date of the meeting. Homeowner’s are encouraged to attend each meeting. “Homeowner Time” is available to allow any resident to bring up any issues, provide feedback on a topic or ask questions.
